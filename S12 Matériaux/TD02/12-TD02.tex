$D_{2(120\degree C)}=D_{2(20\degree C)}.(1-\alpha.(T-T_0))=40,01.(1+23.10^{-6}.100)=39,918$

$S_{(120\degree C)}=\frac{40,098-39,918}{2}=0,09$
